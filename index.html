Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment for a blood test? +

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to minimize your wait time. Certain specialized tests may require a scheduled slot. You can book easily through our contact form or by calling us directly.

How and when will I receive my results? +

Most routine test results are available within 24 hours. You will receive an email and an SMS notification with a link to our secure online portal where you can view and download your reports. Your referring doctor will also receive a copy automatically.

Is fasting required for my test? +

Fasting is required for certain tests, such as a lipid profile or a fasting blood sugar test. This typically means no food or drink (except water) for 8-12 hours prior to your test. Please confirm with your doctor or our staff if your specific test requires fasting.
